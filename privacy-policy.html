How We Collect and Use Information :

We are mainly in the business of compiling business contact information that is publicly available. We collect this information only through legal means and our collection and use of that information is subject to legal entities within the meaning of the General Data Protection Regulation of the European Union and similar United Kingdom laws (all, “GDPR”). We will retain personal information only if necessary in order to achieve those purposes.

We will collect personal information in legal and impartial ways and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the person concerned. Once we have collected Business Information about a person or company, we combine multiple individual or company references into a Public Profile. The Community Profiles profile (“list”) is then provided to Site users and our customers and strategic partners.

Personal data must be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used, and, to the extent required for those purposes, it must be accurate, complete, and up-to-date. We will protect personal information about reasonable protection from loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification. We will make available to our customers information about our personal information policies and procedures.

Security & Encryption :

Every application submitted to our Website is protected by standard SSL (Secure Socket Layer). This means that the pages you view in your shopping cart or anywhere else on our website should be kept confidential and protected from unauthorized access.
